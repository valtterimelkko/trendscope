# Lead Magnet Architect

## Purpose

Design lead magnets that generate qualified leads, not just email addresses. This skill applies the "Value Exchange Economics" principle: the perceived utility must significantly outweigh the cost of providing contact information.

## Prerequisites

Before running this skill, ensure:
- `docs/concept/master-concept.md` exists (JTBD, target persona)
- `marketing/positioning-angles.md` exists (from Phase 1.4.1)
- `marketing/keyword-research.md` exists (from Phase 1.4.2)

## Output

Creates: `marketing/lead-magnet.md`

## Process

### Phase 1: Audience Analysis

Extract from Master Concept:

1. **Primary Persona**
   - Role/title
   - Daily frustrations
   - Goals and aspirations
   - Information consumption preferences

2. **Buyer Awareness Stage**
   - Unaware: Doesn't know they have a problem
   - Problem-Aware: Knows the pain, not the solutions
   - Solution-Aware: Knows solutions exist, comparing options
   - Product-Aware: Knows your product, evaluating fit

3. **Value Currency**
   What does this persona value most?
   - Time savings
   - Cost reduction
   - Risk mitigation
   - Competitive advantage
   - Career advancement

### Phase 2: Format Selection

Select appropriate formats based on awareness stage and persona:

#### Class A: Downloadable Utility (Time Savers)

| Format | Best For | Effort | Intent Level |
|--------|----------|--------|--------------|
| **Swipe File** | Creatives needing inspiration | Low | Low |
| **Cheat Sheet** | Practitioners needing reference | Low | Low-Medium |
| **Checklist/SOP** | Operators needing process | Medium | Medium |
| **Template** | Makers needing starting point | Medium | Medium |
| **Case Study Collection** | Decision makers needing proof | High | Medium-High |

#### Class B: Interactive Mechanisms (Engagement Engines)

| Format | Best For | Effort | Intent Level |
|--------|----------|--------|--------------|
| **ROI Calculator** | Executives building business case | Medium | High |
| **Maturity Assessment** | Leaders benchmarking team | High | High |
| **Audit Tool** | Operators diagnosing problems | Very High | Very High |
| **Comparison Matrix** | Buyers in consideration phase | Medium | High |
| **AI Generator** | Users needing quick output | Medium | Medium |

#### Class C: Educational Curricula (Authority Builders)

| Format | Best For | Effort | Intent Level |
|--------|----------|--------|--------------|
| **Industry Report** | Strategists needing benchmarks | Very High | Medium |
| **Email Course** | Learners wanting structured content | Medium | Medium |
| **Webinar/Masterclass** | Deep learners, high engagement | High | Medium-High |
| **Book/Guide** | Thought leadership seekers | Very High | Low-Medium |
| **Certification** | Career-driven professionals | Very High | High |

#### Class D: Community & Access

| Format | Best For | Effort | Intent Level |
|--------|----------|--------|--------------|
| **Private Community** | Networkers seeking peers | High (ongoing) | Medium |
| **Beta Access** | Early adopters, innovators | Low | High |
| **Resource Library** | Content consumers | Medium | Low |

#### Class E: Service-Led

| Format | Best For | Effort | Intent Level |
|--------|----------|--------|--------------|
| **Strategy Call** | High-ticket prospects | High (human time) | Very High |
| **Done-For-You Service** | Friction-averse buyers | High | Very High |

### Phase 3: Hook Design

Apply the 5-Lever Framework for magnetic hooks:

#### Lever 1: Specificity
- Bad: "Increase your sales"
- Good: "The exact 3-step script we used to close $100k in Q4"

#### Lever 2: Urgency/Relevance
- Tie to external deadlines or timely events
- Example: "2026 Tax Compliance Checklist" (fixed deadline)

#### Lever 3: Credibility Signals
- Numbers: "Used by 500+ SaaS founders"
- Authority: "From a YC-backed team"
- Proof: "The framework behind $10M in ARR"

#### Lever 4: Curiosity Gaps
- Create knowledge deficit that demands resolution
- Example: "The #1 Mistake SaaS Founders Make (That Costs $50k)"

#### Lever 5: Perceived Value
- Make it feel like a "steal"
- Example: "The $500 consulting framework, free"

### Phase 4: Congruence Check

**Critical:** The lead magnet must solve the *immediate predecessor problem* to your paid solution.

| If You Sell... | Good Lead Magnet | Bad Lead Magnet |
|----------------|------------------|-----------------|
| Accounting Software | Invoice Template | Sales Hiring Guide |
| SEO Tool | Keyword Research Checklist | Logo Design Tips |
| Project Management | Sprint Planning Template | Office Decor Ideas |

Ask: "Does consuming this naturally lead to wanting our product?"

### Phase 5: Spec Creation

For each recommended lead magnet, create detailed spec:

```markdown
## Lead Magnet Spec: [Name]

### Overview
- **Format:** [from taxonomy]
- **Target Awareness:** [stage]
- **Intent Level:** [Low/Medium/High/Very High]
- **Production Effort:** [Low/Medium/High/Very High]

### The Hook
- **Headline:** [6-12 words, applying 5 levers]
- **Subheadline:** [clarifying value prop]
- **Credibility Signal:** [proof element]

### Content Outline
1. [Section 1 - what it covers]
2. [Section 2 - what it covers]
3. [Section 3 - what it covers]
...

### Target Keywords
[From keyword research that this magnet targets]

### Funnel Placement
- **Traffic Source:** [where prospects come from]
- **Landing Page URL:** [proposed path]
- **Thank You Page Next Step:** [secondary CTA]

### Nurture Sequence
- **Email 0 (Immediate):** Delivery + one key insight
- **Email 1 (Day 1):** "Did you see this?" - highlight specific section
- **Email 2 (Day 3):** Problem agitation
- **Email 3 (Day 5):** Strategic pivot to product
- **Email 4 (Day 7):** Social proof + soft CTA

### Success Metrics
- Landing Page Conversion: Target [X]% (benchmark: 10-25%)
- Email Open Rate: Target [X]%
- Lead-to-Opportunity Rate: Target [X]% (benchmark: 12-20%)

### Implementation Notes
[Specific guidance for creating this asset]
```

### Phase 6: Validation Protocol

Define smoke test before building:

```markdown
## Validation Plan

### Option A: Cold Outreach Test
- Message: "I'm building [description]. Would you want early access?"
- Target: 50-100 prospects matching ICP
- Platform: LinkedIn DM / Cold Email
- Success Metric: >10% positive reply rate

### Option B: Landing Page Test
- Create simple landing page with hook
- Drive $100-200 in paid traffic
- Success Metric:
  - LinkedIn CTR > 1%
  - Google CTR > 2%
  - Landing page conversion > 10%

### Go/No-Go Decision
- If validation passes: Build full asset
- If validation fails: Test alternative hook or format
```

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

1. **Vanity Downloads** - Optimizing for volume over quality
2. **Generic Topics** - "7 Tips for Sales" vs "7 Cold Scripts for CFO Outreach"
3. **Misalignment** - Lead magnet unrelated to product offering
4. **Dead Ends** - No nurture sequence after download
5. **Over-Engineering** - Building complex tools before validating hooks
