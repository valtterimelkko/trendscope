## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive keyword research strategy for Trendscope, a TikTok trend intelligence platform targeting creators and agencies. Using the **6 Circles Method**, we've identified keyword clusters across the entire search landscape to drive organic traffic and capture high-intent users at every funnel stage.

**Key Findings:**
- **Primary Opportunity:** Mid-volume, high-intent keywords in the "trend intelligence" category with lower competition than generic "TikTok analytics"
- **Sweet Spot:** "TikTok trend alerts," "viral sound detection," and "trend forecasting" keywords
- **Competitive Gap:** Agency-focused keywords (white-label, client reporting) are underserved
