# TikTok Trend Tool That Catches Viral Waves Before They Peak

**Badges:** B2B SaaS · Product/Market · +13 More

---

## Problem Statement

Scrolling is not research. But for millions of TikTok creators, it's all they've got. Sounds blow up and die in the same news cycle. Formats spread across ten thousand videos before lunch and burn out by dinner. Agencies miss opportunities because their process can't keep pace with a platform that treats yesterday's trend like last year's news. Speed wins on TikTok (and every other short-form platform).

---

## Solution: Viral Waves

Viral Waves finds the signal in the noise. It watches the micro-influencer layer where trends start, flagging sounds that surge among small accounts hours before they saturate, formats that jump between niches overnight, and hashtag clusters forming around styles that don't exist 48 hours ago. Alerts land with enough context to act on. Creators move while the window is open. Agencies stop assigning someone to scroll.

---

## The Insight

The insight that makes this work: **velocity matters more than volume.** A sound with 500 uses growing at 400% per hour is a better signal than one with 50,000 uses that peaked yesterday.

Build on TikTok's public APIs and scrape trending data feeds for sound usage, hashtag co-occurrence, and visual similarity. Layer alert logic around growth rate thresholds and niche clustering. Deliver through SMS, email, or Slack. Test with 50 creators and iterate based on which alerts they act on and which they ignore.
