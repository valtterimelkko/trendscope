# 10 Marketing Skills for Claude Code

A unified skill set combining The Vibe Marketer and The Boring Marketer methodologies.

1. **Brand Voice**
   Codify your writing style across future assets, websites, and landing pages. One file ensures every piece of copy matches your voice, not generic AI-speak.

2. **Positioning Angles**
   Find the unique value proposition that makes something sell. 8 frameworks to surface differentiated hooks before you write a word.

3. **Keyword Research**
   Strategic content planning without expensive tools. 6 Circles Method to find SEO/geo topics to write about and prioritize what drives traffic.

4. **Lead Magnet**
   Create opt-in offers people actually want. Research mechanisms to generate emails with hooks, formats, and validation so your free thing does real work.

5. **Direct Response Copy**
   Write powerful hooks and copy that persuades and influences. Landing pages, ads, sales pages that convert using architecture + persuasion principles, not templates.

6. **SEO Content**
   Create high quality articles that rank and drive organic visits. Structure for search engines, voice for people—articles that rank AND sound human.

7. **Newsletter**
   Create ongoing communication with prospects and customers. 9 formats from curated to story-driven. Turn content into recurring audience touchpoints.

8. **Email Sequences**
   The emails between 'subscribed' and 'purchased.' Welcome, nurture, conversion, launch sequences that create powerful drip campaigns.

9. **Content Atomizer**
   Turn 1 piece into 15. Repurpose newsletters and articles into video scripts and social posts. Extract hooks, transform for platforms, maintain voice across channels.

10. **Orchestrator**
    The router that guides the workflow. Asks qualifying questions, diagnoses what you need, and sequences skills in the right order.

Each of the skills should be based on the industry best practice and lots of examples of that ability being used in the industry successfully.
